The terminal can be a scary place for newcomers. It's just a black box with a blinking cursor.

But once you learn the basic commands, you realize it's actually the most powerful way to interact with your computer.

### The Basics

The first thing you need to know is `ls`. This command lists all the files in your current directory.

```bash
$ ls -la
total 16
drwxr-xr-x   4 user  staff  128 May  3 10:00 .
drwxr-xr-x  10 user  staff  320 May  3 10:00 ..
-rw-r--r--   1 user  staff   24 May  3 10:00 file.txt
```
